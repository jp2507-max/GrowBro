# Profile Settings Help

## Overview

Your GrowBro profile is your identity in the community. You can customize your display name, avatar, bio, and privacy settings.

## Getting Started

1. **Open Settings**
   - Tap the **Settings** icon in the app navigation
   - Or use deep link: `growbro://settings`

2. **Navigate to Profile**
   - Tap **Profile** in the settings menu
   - Or use deep link: `growbro://settings/profile`

## Editing Your Profile

### Display Name

Your display name appears next to your posts and comments in the community.

**To change your display name:**

1. Tap the **Display Name** field
2. Enter your new name (3-30 characters)
3. Tap **Save**

**Rules:**

- Must be 3-30 characters long
- Letters, numbers, spaces, hyphens, and underscores allowed
- Profanity will be automatically filtered

### Bio

Your bio is a short description about yourself and your growing experience.

**To add or edit your bio:**

1. Tap the **Bio** field
2. Write your bio (max 500 characters)
3. Tap **Save**

**Tips:**

- Share your growing style (indoor, outdoor, hydroponic)
- Mention your experience level
- Keep it friendly and informative

### Location

Optionally share your general location to connect with local growers.

**To set your location:**

1. Tap the **Location** field
2. Enter your city or region (e.g., "California" or "Berlin")
3. Tap **Save**

**Privacy Note:** Only share what you're comfortable with. You can leave this blank.

## Profile Picture (Avatar)

### Uploading an Avatar

**From Camera:**

1. Tap the avatar circle with camera icon
2. Select **Take Photo**
3. Take your photo
4. Review and confirm

**From Photo Library:**

1. Tap the avatar circle
2. Select **Choose from Library**
3. Select your photo
4. Review and confirm

### Image Requirements

- **Format:** JPG or PNG
- **Size:** Will be automatically cropped to square (1:1 aspect ratio)
- **Resolution:** Resized to 512x512 pixels
- **File Size:** Compressed to under 200KB

### Privacy & Security

- **EXIF Removal:** All metadata (GPS location, camera info, timestamps) is automatically removed
- **Center Crop:** Images are cropped from the center to preserve subject
- **Quality:** Images are compressed while maintaining visual quality

### Upload Status

While uploading, you'll see a progress indicator:

- **10%:** Removing metadata
- **20%:** Analyzing dimensions
- **40%:** Cropping to square
- **50%:** Compressing image
- **70%:** Uploading to server
- **100%:** Complete!

**If upload fails:**

- Check your internet connection
- Try a smaller image
- Ensure the image is not corrupted
- Tap **Retry** to try again

## Privacy Settings

### Show Profile to Community

Control whether your profile is visible to other users.

**Enabled (Default):**

- Your profile appears in community searches
- Other users can view your posts and profile
- You can receive follow requests

**Disabled:**

- Your profile is hidden from searches
- Only your posts are visible (without profile link)
- You cannot be followed

**To change visibility:**

1. Toggle **Show profile to community**
2. Changes apply immediately

## Offline Functionality

### What Works Offline

- View your current profile
- Edit profile fields (display name, bio, location)
- View account statistics
- Toggle privacy settings

### What Requires Internet

- Avatar upload
- Saving profile changes to the cloud
- Viewing other users' profiles

### Sync Behavior

When you make changes offline:

1. **Local Save:** Changes are saved immediately on your device
2. **Queue for Sync:** Changes are queued for upload
3. **Auto-Sync:** When internet returns, changes sync automatically
4. **Retry Logic:** Failed syncs retry with increasing delays (1s, 2s, 4s, 8s, 16s, 30s)
5. **Error Handling:** You'll see an error message if sync permanently fails

## Troubleshooting

### Avatar Upload Failed

**Problem:** Avatar upload keeps failing

**Solutions:**

1. Check your internet connection
2. Try a smaller image (under 5MB original size)
3. Ensure the image is valid (JPG or PNG)
4. Close and reopen the app
5. Contact support if problem persists

### Profile Not Syncing

**Problem:** Changes aren't appearing on other devices

**Solutions:**

1. Ensure you're connected to the internet
2. Pull down to refresh on the other device
3. Check for sync errors in the sync status bar
4. Tap **Retry** on any error messages
5. Sign out and sign in again if problem persists

### Statistics Not Updating

**Problem:** Account statistics seem outdated

**Solutions:**

1. Pull down to refresh the screen
2. Wait 1-2 seconds for cache to update
3. Check if you have pending syncs in the sync status
4. Restart the app if statistics still don't update

### Profanity Filter Too Strict

**Problem:** Your display name or bio is being rejected incorrectly

**Solutions:**

1. Try alternative wording
2. Avoid abbreviations that might be flagged
3. Contact support to review the filter
4. Use full words instead of shortened versions
